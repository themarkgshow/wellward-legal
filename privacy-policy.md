## What We Collect
- **Account Data:** Name and email via Sign in with Apple (we receive only a 
  relay email address — Apple never shares your real email unless you choose to)
- **Recovery Data:** Sobriety dates, journal entries, mood logs stored locally 
  on your device and synced via iCloud (CloudKit) — only you can access this
- **Usage Analytics:** Anonymous, non-identifiable app usage via TelemetryDeck
- **AI Conversations:** Messages sent to the AI Coach are processed by OpenAI's 
  API and are subject to OpenAI's privacy policy. We do not store conversation 
  history on our servers.

## What We Do NOT Collect
- We do not sell your data to anyone, ever
- We do not share your recovery data with advertisers
- We do not collect device identifiers for tracking
- We do not store payment information (handled entirely by Apple)

## How Your Data Is Stored
All personal recovery data is stored in iCloud CloudKit under your Apple ID.
Anthropic (Claude) and OpenAI have separate privacy policies governing 
AI interactions.

## Your Rights
You may delete your account and all associated data at any time from 
Settings → Account → Delete Account within the app.
