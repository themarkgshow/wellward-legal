Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my sobriety date?
Go to your Profile tab → tap "Reset Sobriety Date." You can choose to keep your previous journey as a record or start completely fresh. There is no shame in resetting — every day is a new beginning.
My AI Coach isn't responding. What do I do?
Make sure you have an active internet connection. The AI Coach requires connectivity to generate responses. If you're connected and still seeing issues try closing and reopening the app. If the problem persists contact us below.
How do I restore my purchase?
Open the app → go to the Premium tab → tap "Restore Purchases" at the bottom of the screen. Make sure you're signed in to the same Apple ID you used to purchase.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Subscriptions are managed through Apple. Go to iPhone Settings → tap your name → Subscriptions → Wellward → Cancel Subscription. You'll retain premium access until the end of your current billing period.
Is my data private?
Yes. All your personal data is stored privately in your iCloud account. Wellward does not have access to your journal entries, mood logs, or sobriety data. We never sell your data. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
I deleted the app. Is my data gone?
No. Your data is stored in iCloud and will be restored automatically when you reinstall Wellward and sign in with the same Apple ID.
How do I add a widget to my home screen?
Long press your iPhone home screen → tap the + button in the top corner → search for Wellward → choose your widget size → tap Add Widget.
The Treatment Finder isn't showing results near me.
Make sure you've granted Wellward location access in iPhone Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services → Wellward → While Using the App. If location is enabled and results aren't showing your area may have limited SAMHSA-listed facilities — try expanding your search radius.
