Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Wellward is built on a foundation of trust. Your recovery journey is deeply personal and your privacy is our highest priority. This policy explains exactly what data we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

1. Data We Collect

Wellward collects only what is necessary to provide the app's features:

  • Sobriety data — your start date, substance type, and milestone history
  • Journal entries — text you write in the journal feature
  • Mood logs — mood ratings you record
  • Craving events — craving intensity, triggers, and outcomes you log
  • AI Coach messages — conversations with the AI coach
  • Community posts — content you choose to share publicly
  • Notification preferences — your notification settings

2. How Your Data Is Stored

All personal data is stored privately in your iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit framework. This means:

  • Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest by Apple
  • Only you can access your private data
  • Wellward does not operate its own database of user records
  • Your data syncs automatically across all your Apple devices
  • Deleting the app does not delete your iCloud data — you can restore it if you reinstall

3. AI Coach & Cloudflare

The AI Coach feature sends your messages to an AI language model to generate responses. This is routed through a secure Cloudflare Worker proxy. Your messages are processed to generate a response and are not stored by Wellward or used for AI training purposes. No personally identifiable information is attached to AI requests.

4. Community Features

Content you post to the Community feed is stored in CloudKit's public database and is visible to other Wellward users. Posts are anonymous by default. Do not include personally identifiable information in community posts.

5. Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name or email address
  • We do not collect payment information — all purchases are handled by Apple
  • We do not sell your data to any third party
  • We do not use your data for advertising
  • We do not share your data with insurance companies, employers, or any third party

6. Analytics

Wellward uses privacy-first analytics to understand general app usage patterns such as which features are used most often. No personally identifiable information is included in analytics data.

7. Children's Privacy

Wellward is rated 17+ and is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 17. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

8. Your Rights

You can delete all your Wellward data at any time by deleting your iCloud data through iOS Settings → Apple ID → iCloud → Manage Storage → Wellward. You may also contact us to request data deletion.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent update.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or your data please contact us at support@fusionflock.ai
